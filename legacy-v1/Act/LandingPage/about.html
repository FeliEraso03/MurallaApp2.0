We provide optimal evacuation routes

The Historic Center of Cartagena de Indias is an architectural and cultural gem that attracts thousands of tourists each year. However, this area faces significant challenges in emergency management due to its high population density and the complexity of its road infrastructure. The narrow streets and large number of people can hinder rapid and safe evacuations in emergency situations such as fires, earthquakes, or floods.

Importance of the Project

The development of a web application that determines the best evacuation routes in the Historic Center is essential for several reasons:

● Protection of Lives In emergency situations, every second counts. The ability to evacuate people quickly can make the difference between life and death.

● Resource Optimization: Authorities can use the application to better plan and coordinate evacuation efforts, optimizing the use of resources such as emergency personnel and vehicles.

● Reduction of Congestion: The application can help reduce congestion on major roads, allowing for a smoother and faster evacuation.
