About the Project

We provide optimal evacuation routes

The MURALLAGRAPH project is designed to help residents and tourists of Cartagena de Indias Find the fastest and safest evacuation routes in case of emergency. Using Advanced graph techniques and an interactive Leaflet map, this tool provides information crucial in real time to ensure everyone's safety.

The historic center of Cartagena de Indias, with its complex network of streets and its high flow of requires innovative solutions for emergency management. MURALLAGRAPH integrates data real-time and optimization algorithms to provide the best evacuation routes, dynamically adapting to changing conditions.

Characteristics

Interactive Map

Use Leaflet to view and navigate the downtown map historical of Cartagena de Indias.

Graph Management

Easily create, edit, and remove nodes and edges to design optimal evacuation routes.

Built-in algorithms

It runs algorithms like Dijkstra and Ford-Fulkerson directly in The map.
